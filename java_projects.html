Markov Model: A.I. that Generates Text

The Markov Model is a probabilistic model that encodes information by storing different states. This model notices the changes of states and predicts the outcome of these transitional states. By training the A.I. through text, it is able to notice the probability and occurence of a word and predict the next word from a specified text. By training this model with more text, the A.I. is able to mimic any text and will have the capacity to fool a human being.
